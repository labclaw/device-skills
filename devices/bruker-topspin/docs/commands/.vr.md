# *2, *8, /2, /8, .vr, .all

**Category:** Commands > Miscellaneous > Graphical User Interface

## NAME

***2** - Increase the Intensity by a Factor of 2 (1D, 2D, 3D)

***8** - Increase the Intensity by a Factor of 8 (1D, 2D, 3D)

**/2** - Decrease the Intensity by a Factor of 2 (1D, 2D, 3D)

**/8** - Decrease the Intensity by a Factor of 8 (1D, 2D, 3D)

**.vr** - Reset the Intensity (1D, 2D, 3D)

**.all** - Reset Horizontal and Vertical Scaling (1D, 2D, 3D)


## DESCRIPTION

These commands increase, decrease, or reset the vertical scaling of a dataset.
